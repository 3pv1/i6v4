Proxy

Setting a Proxy leads to tunneling all network communication (except the Bypassed Domains) through it; including https, http, ftp and ws connections.

Stealth Instance

Setting the Stealth instance allows to use another machine than the local one. The Stealth instance is defaulted with localhost.

Bypassed Domains

The Bypassed Domains are a whitelist that leads to bypassing the Proxy setting.

You should only carefully whitelist domains that you absolutely trust, as this will compromise and leak your identity with those domains and make you traceable.
